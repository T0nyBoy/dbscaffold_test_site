About Davis & Barrett

Scaffolding you can rely on

We've been putting up scaffolding on residential sites for housebuilders and developers across the region. Every job gets a proper look before we start, a team that turns up when they say they will, and a scaffold that's signed off and ready for the trades waiting on it.

  • Scaffolds erected by trained, experienced crews
  • Designs checked against TG20:21 guidance, with bespoke drawings when a job needs them
  • Covered by public liability insurance
  • Weekly scaffold inspections, logged and handed over
